Our Mission

Supporting Veterans' Recovery Journey

Our mission is to assure veterans who suffer from Substance Abuse and Mental Health issues have access to a full continuum of care, ensuring a seamless care continuum based on their individualizing situation, support, and care needs.

Our support ranges from transitioning outpatient treatment to vocational rehabilitation and post-treatment.

Our Vision

Lasting Recovery and Resilience

S & S Veterans Services, LLC is a for-profit organization that provides Veterans in Prince Williams County (PWC), Virginia, with assistance eradicating those struggling with alcohol and Drug abuse, Mental illness, and financial planning.

Our primary service is to provide veterans with an individualized treatment plan to help them regain self-sufficiency (or self-resilience) and overcome barriers through a holistic treatment plan.

Comprehensive Services for Lasting Well-being

Explore our offerings and embark on a journey towards a brighter, more self-sufficient future.

Comprehensive Assessment

Each veteran will undergo a comprehensive assessment to identify their needs and develop an individualized treatment plan.

Virtual Counseling sessions

Individual counseling sessions will address issues related to addiction, mental health, post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD), depression, anxiety, and other co-occurring disorders.

Financial literacy

We will assist veterans in understanding how to save and invest their finances.

Evidence-Based Treatment

We will offer evidence-based treatments such as cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT), dialectical behavior therapy (DBT), medication-assisted treatment (MAT), and trauma-focused therapies.

Family Support

We recognize the importance of family involvement in the recovery process. We will provide educational programs and support services to help families understand and cope with the challenges faced by their loved ones.
